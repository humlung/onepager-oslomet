What is Namewhere?

NameWhere creates clothes that love nature for people who love nature. 100% recycled and renewable materials and 0% environmental damange during production. We're all about being vegan as it results in no environmental impact. Non-vegan products generally require more resources and cause higher greenhouse gas emissions

What are our principles?

We strongly believe that several measures need to be taken in order to ensure sustainable. We list the following requirements for our production:

  • All creatures are ensured life and freedom
  • No living creature should suffer psychological or physical stress
  • No water wasted
  • No use of chemicals

About us

Loving nature is what we do. However, the northern climate is harsh and not always loving towards us humans who wish to get out in nature. Therefore, solid gear is of essence so bad weather is no hindrance but remains just that; bad weather.

As of now, we are two people sewing and testing out our prototype clothing at the west coast of Norway. However, our destination is clear: Being able to equip all nature-loving humans with the most nature-loving clothing there is to find.
